About Us

Location: Atwima Trabuom
Our focus is on sustainable pig production with the goal of addressing poverty, promoting climate action, creating employment opportunities, increasing food security, supporting economic growth, fostering community development, and promoting gender equality. We strive to ensure a consistent supply of pigs throughout the month to meet market demands, which can help to stabilize prices and support small-scale pig farmers. Our sustainable pig production practices also have the potential to mitigate the impact of climate change by reducing greenhouse gas emissions and improving soil health through responsible manure management. By generating employment opportunities in the pig production sector, we aim to contribute to economic growth and community development. Furthermore, our commitment to gender equality ensures that all members of our team, regardless of gender, are given equal opportunities and support to thrive.

Sarbilles Farms has currently hired two workers, one male and one female. Prior to starting work, these workers are given the necessary education and training. By providing education and training to workers, Sarbilles Farms aims to ensure that they have the skills and knowledge required to perform their duties effectively and safely. This can lead to higher levels of productivity, better quality work, and a reduced risk of workplace accidents. In addition, by offering equal employment opportunities to both male and female workers, Sarbilles Farms is actively promoting gender equality in the workplace. This helps to foster a more diverse and inclusive work environment, where all employees have an equal opportunity to contribute and succeed. Overall, Sarbilles Farms' emphasis on education and gender equality is a positive step towards building a strong and sustainable workforce.
